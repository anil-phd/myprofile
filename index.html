ABOUT

After leading a wonderful team and building AI/ML platforms for market research at Graphene AI, I have started working as Scientist at IHPC, AStar. I graduated from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore under the supervision of Dr. Erik Cambria with a Ph.D. degree with specialisation on Natural language understanding and Opinion Mining. I have been working in the field of Natural Language Processing and Sentiment Analysis for 6 years. I am open to collaboration and mentoring students. Visit my Google Scholar for full list of publications.
